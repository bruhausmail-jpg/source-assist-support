Azul Score Keeper Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Azul Score Keeper is designed as a simple score tracking companion app. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

Information the app stores

The app may store game data locally on your device, including player names, round scores, game history, and your dark mode preference. This information stays on your device unless you choose to share results using your device’s share options.

Information we collect

Azul Score Keeper does not require an account and does not collect personal information through the app.

Sharing results

If you use the Share Results feature, your device will open the system share sheet. You choose what app or person receives the shared score summary. Azul Score Keeper does not receive or store that sharing activity.

Third-party services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or external databases.

Children’s privacy

The app is a general-purpose score keeping tool and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Data deletion

You can delete saved games using the app’s history controls. You can also remove all locally stored data by deleting the app from your device.

Unofficial companion app

Azul Score Keeper is an unofficial companion app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the publishers or rights holders of Azul.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: support@azulscorekeeper.app
