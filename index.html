Source Assist Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Source Assist respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may use, how it is used, and how we protect your information.

Information We Collect

Source Assist does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal profile information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.

The app may use your device location, if you allow location access, to help find nearby thrift stores, garage sales, estate sales, and related sourcing opportunities.

Location Information

Source Assist uses your location only to provide location-based search results, map results, route planning, and directions. Location information is used while you are using the app and is not sold.

You may disable location access at any time through your device settings. Some app features may not work correctly without location access.

Third-Party Services

Source Assist may use third-party services to provide search results, maps, directions, and sourcing information. These services may include, but are not limited to:

  • Apple Maps
  • Google Maps
  • Google Places
  • Craigslist
  • Estate sale listing sources

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Source Assist may store limited app preferences on your device, such as selected map preferences or app settings. This information is used only to improve your app experience.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Source Assist may receive basic diagnostic or crash information from Apple or app platform services to help improve reliability and fix bugs. This information is not used to personally identify users.

Subscriptions and Payments

If paid features or subscriptions are added, purchases will be handled through Apple’s App Store payment system. Source Assist does not directly collect or store credit card information.

Children’s Privacy

Source Assist is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and remove it if needed.

How We Use Information

Information used by Source Assist is used to:

  • Find nearby sourcing locations
  • Display maps and directions
  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and improve reliability
  • Remember app preferences

Information Sharing

Source Assist does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to operate app features, comply with legal requirements, or use third-party services required by the app.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
